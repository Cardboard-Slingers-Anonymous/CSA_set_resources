## Standard Booster Pack Structure

Every booster pack in MTG Arena contains **8 cards**:

| Slot | Rarity |
|---|---|
| 1–5 | Common |
| 6–7 | Uncommon |
| 8 | Rare or Mythic Rare* |

\* The Rare slot may upgrade to a Mythic Rare at a set-specific rate (see below).

## Wildcard Drop Rates

Wildcards can be redeemed for any card of the same rarity.

| Rarity | Expected Rate (per pack) |
|---|---|
| Common Wildcard | 1:3 packs |
| Uncommon Wildcard | 1:5 packs |
| Rare Wildcard | 1:30 packs |
| Mythic Wildcard | 1:30 packs |

### Wildcard Track
- Every **6 packs** opened earns a guaranteed Wildcard Reward (WCR).
- Uncommon track starts with 3 progress, so the first Uncommon WCR comes after the 3rd pack.
- The Rare/Mythic track awards **4 Rare WCRs** before each **1 Mythic WCR**.

## Golden Packs

Earned for every **10 packs purchased** from Standard-legal and/or Alchemy-legal sets.

Each Golden Pack contains **6 Rares or Mythic Rares**:
- At least **1 guaranteed Mythic Rare**
- **3 cards** from any Standard-legal set (Rare or Mythic)
- **2 cards** from the most recent Standard-legal set
- Rare → Mythic upgrade rate: **~1:7.3**

> Golden Packs do not grant Wildcards but do advance the Wildcard Track.

## Duplicate Protection

### Store Packs
- If a Rare/Mythic pulled already has 4 copies owned, it is **replaced** with a different card of the same rarity from the same set.
- If the **entire set is complete**: replaced with **20 Gems** (Rare) or **40 Gems** (Mythic).

### Limited Packs (Draft/Sealed)
- Duplicate protection is **not** applied during pack opening for picks.
- After the event, extra Rares/Mythics → Gems; extra Commons/Uncommons → Vault progress.

### Individual Card Rewards (ICRs)
- Extra Rare ICR: **20 Gems**
- Extra Mythic ICR: **40 Gems**
- Extra Uncommon ICR: goes toward the Vault.

## Vault Rewards

The Vault fills when players acquire a 5th+ copy of a Common or Uncommon card.

| Card Rarity | Vault Points per Extra Copy |
|---|---|
| Common | 1 point |
| Uncommon | 3 points |

At **1000 Vault Points**, the Vault opens and grants:
- 1 Mythic Wildcard
- 2 Rare Wildcards
- 3 Uncommon Wildcards
